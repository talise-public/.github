# Talise

**Money that moves like a message.**

A gasless US dollar account on Sui. Send by name, settle in under a second, keep the amount private. Live on mainnet.

[Website](https://talise.io) · [Overview and pitch](https://github.com/talise-public/talise-docs) · [X](https://x.com/taliseio)

</div>

---

### What we are building

Talise is a dollar account that lives inside a conversation. Sign in with Google, hold dollars, and send them to `name@talise.sui` instead of a 0x address. No seed phrase, no gas, no waiting. Private sends hide the amount on chain.

### Repositories

- **[talise-frontend](https://github.com/talise-public/talise-frontend)** — web app and API. Next.js, TypeScript.

### Built on Sui

zkLogin for identity, sponsored gas for fees, sub-second finality for settlement, and a Groth16 shielded pool for privacy.
